Chronicle: A Historical Database of ETH Events

This project aims to collect all information about ETH community events such as hackathons or conferences. Each ETH event is unique and has its own specifics, and we would like to preserve this diversity as an inspiration for future organizers.

Database

The entire database is available as a single JSON: - chronicle.ethevents.club/data/index.json

Frontends

You can use this sites to browse our database: - ethevents.xyz (source)

Contribute!

We will be glad to receive any additions or corrections to our events data. Feel free to do PR!
